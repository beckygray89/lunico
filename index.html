Ladies & Gents hairdresser in Peterborough

Our professional and experienced team of hairdressers use the latest hair cutting and styling techniques for men and women to deliver the results you want. Get people talking – and start loving your hair.

We want you to love your hair, so we create beautiful haircuts that last longer than just a day. We give you a hair style that is easy to maintain, suits your lifestyle, your personality and looks fantastic. We are renowned for our high quality and skillful work.

Based in Farct, Peterborough. Lunico salon has been established since 2006. Since then, we’ve gained an enviable reputation for our high quality hairdressing and our excellent, highly rated customer service. Take a look at our most recent 5-star customer reviews on our Facebook page below.
